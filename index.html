The Vision

1bit.sbs is the parent domain and the creative heartbeat for a series of upcoming "vibe-coded" projects. In a world of rigid structures and dry technicalities, this space exists to explore the intersection of technology, aesthetics, and pure intuition.

This isn't just about building software; it’s about crafting digital experiences that resonate. Every project launched under this banner is a step toward a future where code feels more like art and less like a chore.

The Mind Behind the Machine

I’m Roshan Ingle. To be honest, I don’t call myself a "technical expert" or a traditional developer. I am a learner, a builder, and a believer in Vibe Coding.

For me, the process is simple: I have a vision for how something should feel, and I use code as the medium to bring that vibe to life. I’m currently in the lab—learning the ropes, breaking things, and rebuilding them—all to ensure that every project under 1bit.sbs carries a unique digital soul.

And here is the best part: I am doing all of this from my 7-year-old Realme 3 Pro phone. Even this very website was vibe-coded entirely on a mobile screen. It’s proof that you don't need high-end gear to build something that feels right.
